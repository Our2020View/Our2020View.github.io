The See Level II tank monitor we installed in the coach to allow us to monitor the fresh water tank contents has been working out really great.  So great, in fact, that I wanted to install another one within view of the fresh water gravity fill port at the rear of the View. 

(If you haven't seen our writeup on the initial tank monitor's installation, you can see it here:)

<a href = "https://www.our2020view.ca/ourmods/seelevel/ " target="_blank">Original See Level II Installation </a>

When filling the freshwater tank from either the gravity fill, or the city water fill in the wet bay, many times I wanted to be to be able to monitor the level as the fill proceeds.  I decided that having a second See Level II display monitor viewable from outside was going to make things much more convenient.

The display installation guide advertises that adding a second display is possible, and reading through the drawings and configuration steps confirmed that it was going to be pretty straighforward.  As usually is the case, it would have been easier to add a second monitor at the same time as I added the origianl system.  I think the hardest part of this second display installation was trying to remember how I wired up the first one!  After going to the original installation's webpage and reading my notes, things came into focus and I was able to make sense of the wiring harness tucked away under the bed.
